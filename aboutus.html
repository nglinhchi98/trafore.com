Our story

Trafore Oy was founded in 2007, specializes in information technology with extensively experienced owners and employees in the field. Our operations focus on business operations in three areas:
Transport system (Exim)
Healthcare system (Babe)
Customized applications with 4D software
We locate in the heart of Helsinki, Finland and impletement Agile Manifesto principles in our business operations.

In the Agile Manifesto, software developers promote the method of customer collaboration so well it was reason enough to be the very first contract I signed in the process of founding Trafore Oy in 2007. Extensive paperwork and heavy contract models can and should always be minimized.
